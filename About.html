About

Thanks for visiting my site! My name is Kristen, and I specialize in creating handcrafted stationery and holiday greeting cards. I take special orders and discount for bulk pricing.

Located in northeast Ohio, I love all arts and crafts. I've found that there is nothing in the world like a hand written note. A thank you card can make or break a job offer, and a simple note can rekindle relationships that have gone by the wayside.

My products are designed for people who want to write a note in a thoughtful way but don't have the time or resources to make something themselves.
