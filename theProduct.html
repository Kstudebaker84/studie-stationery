Products

Studie Stationery creates both pre-made and made-to-order handmade greeting cards. Materials used include paper, corregated card board, fabric, felt, embossing, and 3D embellishments.

Why should you choose to use Studie Stationery products over manufactured greeting cards? Simple. Handmade cards are like snowflakes: unique as you are... And unique as the relationship you have with the person you're planning to give a greeting card.
